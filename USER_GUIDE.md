# TPW User Guide

## 🦋 Welcome to The Purple Wings

The Purple Wings is a comprehensive financial literacy platform designed specifically for women. This guide will help you navigate the platform, access courses, track your progress, and make the most of your learning journey.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

#### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Visit the TPW homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
3. Choose your preferred method:
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Apple ID

#### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
After signing up, you'll be guided through our onboarding process:

**Personal Information:**
- Full name
- Email address (already provided)
- Location (optional)
- Professional information

**Financial Assessment:**
- Current financial knowledge level
- Financial goals and priorities
- Topics you're most interested in
- Available time for learning

**Learning Preferences:**
- Preferred learning style
- Time commitment
- Experience level

### First Login Experience

#### Welcome Wizard
Our interactive onboarding wizard helps you:
- Assess your current financial knowledge
- Identify your learning goals
- Get personalized course recommendations
- Set up your learning schedule

#### Personality Assessment
Take our optional personality assessment to:
- Discover your financial personality type
- Understand your money management style
- Get tailored learning recommendations
- Connect with similar learners

## 📚 Navigating Courses

### Finding Courses

#### Course Catalog
1. Click "Courses" in the main navigation
2. Browse by curriculum or difficulty level
3. Use filters to narrow your search:
   - Skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
   - Topics (Budgeting, Investing, Retirement, etc.)
   - Time commitment
   - Format (Video, Reading, Interactive)

#### Personalized Recommendations
Based on your assessment, you'll see:
- **Top Picks:** Courses most relevant to your goals
- **Quick Wins:** Short courses for immediate impact
- **Deep Dives:** Comprehensive learning paths

### Course Structure

#### Curriculum Overview
Courses are organized into curricula:
- **Financial Foundations:** Basic concepts and skills
- **Investment Mastery:** Advanced investing strategies
- **Retirement Planning:** Preparing for the future
- **Life Stage Planning:** Age-specific financial guidance

#### Lesson Format
Each lesson includes:
- **Video Content:** 5-15 minute instructional videos
- **Reading Materials:** Comprehensive text explanations
- **Interactive Elements:** Quizzes and practical exercises
- **Resources:** Downloadable worksheets and tools

### Starting a Course

#### Enrollment Options
1. **Free Preview:** Sample lessons before committing
2. **Full Enrollment:** Complete access to all course content
3. **Learning Path:** Guided sequence through multiple courses

#### Progress Tracking
- **Visual Progress Bars:** See your completion status
- **Time Tracking:** Monitor study time vs. estimates
- **Achievement Badges:** Earn recognition for milestones
- **Certificates:** Receive completion certificates
