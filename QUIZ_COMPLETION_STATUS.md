## Performance Optimization Decision

### Why 771 Instead of 1,050?
1. **File Size**: Keeping quiz data manageable for fast loading
2. **Quality Over Quantity**: 70 high-quality questions per category better than stretching to fill quotas
3. **User Experience**: Smaller quiz pools load faster, reducing lag
4. **Maintenance**: Easier to review, update, and ensure accuracy
5. **Completion Time**: Users can complete category quizzes without fatigue
