- How to choose based on personal motivation style

**4. Creating Your Personal Debt Payoff Plan**
- Timeline options (aggressive 2-3 years to sustainable 5-7 years)

- Visual progress tracking methods

**5. Avoiding New Debt While Paying Off Old**
- Understanding debt triggers (lifestyle, emergency, emotional, peer pressure)
- Solutions tailored to different root causes
- Credit card strategy during payoff
- The mental shift from "How much can I spend?" to "How much can I save?"
- Building permanent habits, not temporary fixes

**6. Tracking Progress and Staying Motivated**
- Monthly 35-minute check-in ritual
- Milestone celebration planning
- Accountability systems (partners, online communities, professional support)
- Motivation through numbers (interest saved, income gained, time freed)
- Handling slip-ups without abandonment
- What to do once debt-free
