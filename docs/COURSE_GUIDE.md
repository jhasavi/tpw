# TPW Course Guide

## 📚 Course Structure Overview

The Purple Wings platform offers comprehensive financial literacy courses organized into curricula, courses, and individual lessons. This guide explains how courses are structured and how users can navigate their learning journey.

## 🎯 Learning Paths

### Available Curricula

#### 1. Financial Foundations
**Target Audience:** Beginners seeking basic financial literacy
**Prerequisites:** None
**Estimated Time:** 8-12 hours

**Courses:**
- Budgeting Basics
- Saving Strategies
- Understanding Credit
- Introduction to Investing

#### 2. Investment Mastery
**Target Audience:** Intermediate learners
**Prerequisites:** Financial Foundations
**Estimated Time:** 15-20 hours

**Courses:**
- Stock Market Fundamentals
- Portfolio Management
- Risk Assessment
- Advanced Investment Strategies

#### 3. Retirement Planning
**Target Audience:** Adults planning for retirement
**Prerequisites:** Financial Foundations
**Estimated Time:** 10-15 hours

**Courses:**
- Retirement Account Types
- Social Security Planning
- Healthcare in Retirement
- Estate Planning Basics

#### 4. Life Stage Financial Planning
**Target Audience:** All ages
**Prerequisites:** Varies by course
**Estimated Time:** 20-30 hours total

**Courses:**
- Young Adult Finance (20s-30s)
- Family Financial Planning (30s-40s)
- Peak Earning Years (40s-50s)
- Pre-Retirement (50s-60s)

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Assessment
New users complete a comprehensive assessment to determine:
- **Current Knowledge Level:** Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
- **Learning Goals:** Specific financial objectives
- **Time Commitment:** Available study time
- **Interest Areas:** Topics of greatest relevance

### Step 2: Personalized Recommendations
Based on assessment results, users receive:
- **Recommended Courses:** Tailored to skill level and goals
- **Learning Path:** Optimal course sequence
- **Time Estimates:** Realistic completion timelines
- **Priority Rankings:** Most relevant courses first

### Step 3: Course Enrollment
Users can:
- **Enroll Individually:** Select specific courses
- **Follow Recommendations:** Accept suggested path
- **Browse Catalog:** Explore all available options
- **Save for Later:** Build a wishlist
